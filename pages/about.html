About us

Amodo is the dedicated logistics branch of AMO Fresh, focused on delivering fresh, high-quality products to customers with precision and care.

AMO Fresh offers 24/7 access to a wide range of premium fruits and agricultural products for the country's top hotels, restaurants, catering companies, and supermarkets. We ensure an efficient and fast ordering process to meet your business needs. Whether you are running a restaurant chain or managing a high-end hotel, our customer service team is ready to take your orders around the clock.

With our experience, connection to the land, and innovative approach, amofresh is a strong supply chain that ensures quality and trust from the moment the product is grown until it reaches the customer.
