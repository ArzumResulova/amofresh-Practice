Through Modern Agriculture

AmoTrade carries out the wholesale of over 80,000 tons of fruits and vegetables, cooperating with more than 30 countries in both the Azerbaijani and international markets.

Wholesale is the process of purchasing products in large quantities from producers or distributors and selling them to wholesale and retail sellers, supermarket chains, businesses, and other customers. This sales model allows companies to obtain products at more favorable prices and manage the supply chain efficiently.

Advantages:
  • Wide product variety
  • Business stability through large orders
  • Access to diverse markets
